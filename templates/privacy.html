Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 January 2026

1. Data Controller

Security Buddy ("we", "us") operates the web service at securitybuddy.app. We are the data controller for personal data collected through this service. For privacy enquiries, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.

2. Data We Collect

DataWhen collectedStored?
Username, email, hashed password On account registration Yes — until account deletion
Scanned domain or IP address + results On every scan Yes — see retention below
IP address Each request No — processed transiently for rate-limiting and SSRF prevention only
Analytics (page views) With your consent Aggregated — no personal identifiers

3. Legal Basis

ProcessingLegal basis
Account management, scan history Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR — performance of a contract
Analytics cookies Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR — consent (opt-in via banner)
Security rate-limiting Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR — legitimate interests (preventing abuse)

4. Data Retention

  • Account data — retained until you delete your account.
  • Authenticated scan results — retained with your account until deletion.
  • Guest scan results — automatically deleted after 90 days.
  • Analytics data — aggregated; no personal identifiers retained by us. Subject to Vercel's retention policy.

5. Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the following rights under GDPR. To exercise any right, either use the self-service options below or contact us via GitHub.

  • Access (Art. 15) — download all your data from {% if current_user.is_authenticated %} Account → Export data {% else %} your Account Settings after login {% endif %}.
  • Erasure (Art. 17) — delete your account and all data from {% if current_user.is_authenticated %} Account Settings {% else %} Account Settings after login {% endif %}.
  • Portability (Art. 20) — your data export is machine-readable JSON.
  • Rectification (Art. 16) — contact us via GitHub to correct inaccurate data.
  • Object / Restrict (Art. 21/18) — contact us via GitHub.
  • Lodge a complaint (Art. 77) — you may lodge a complaint with your local data protection supervisory authority.

6. Cookies

We set no cookies by default. If you click Accept on the cookie consent banner, Vercel Analytics may set cookies prefixed va- to count page views. No personal data is stored in these cookies.

You can withdraw consent at any time by clearing this site's data in your browser settings, or by clicking Reject on the banner. Consent is stored in localStorage under the key sb_consent.

7. Third-party Services

  • Vercel — our hosting and infrastructure provider, and the provider of Vercel Analytics (if consented). See Vercel's Privacy Policy.
  • Threat intelligence APIs (URLhaus, ThreatFox, MalwareBazaar) — queried when you use the Threat Intel scanner. Only the URL, domain, IP, or hash you submit is sent. No account or personal data is shared.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the service evolves. Material changes will be noted in the GitHub repository. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
